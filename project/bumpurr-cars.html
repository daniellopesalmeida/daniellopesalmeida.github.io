Bumpurr Cars
Overview
This game is a local 4-player bumper car game, where the main goal is to get the most amount of points by the end of the timer. Players can get points by bumping into other players and the faster they bump the more points they get.
Made for the course of Game Projects in the second year of the bachelor degree in Game Development from Digital Arts and Entertainment, Howest. This was a group project composed of six people, 3 programmers and 3 artists and made in Unreal Engine and C++.
My contributions to this project include implementing several gameplay mechanics such as collision intensity based on the foward direction vector of the cars. Hazards, which consisted on periodic events such as spikes, bumper pads like in a pinbal machine and ice floor.
I also contributed to the implementation of the UI, score system and highscore.
